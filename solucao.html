Solução - Roadworks

Enunciado

In an under-construction village, n houses have been built in a row numbered from 1 to n. House i has hospitality hi.

The village has n−1 roads, where road i connects houses i and i+1 and will be built on day di. Initially, no roads are built.

You start at house x and will stay in the village from day 1 to day k, initially with a satisfaction of 0.

On day s, the following happens in order:

  1. All roads i with di = s are built.
  2. You may move to an adjacent house, if the road to it has been built, or stay at your current house.
  3. Your satisfaction increases by hj, where j is the house you are currently at.

Find the maximum satisfaction you can achieve after k days.

🔗 Submeter: Problema no Codeforces

Solução Comentada

Observe que as estradas são construídas ao longo do tempo. Em cada instante existe um intervalo contínuo de casas que pode ser alcançado.

A estratégia consiste em manter a região acessível e determinar qual casa produz a melhor satisfação possível para os dias restantes.

Utilizando estruturas adequadas conseguimos atualizar o intervalo e responder consultas de máximo de forma eficiente.
